10 Best Air Jordan Releases Of 2016

Jordan Brand is already gearing up for a massive 2017 lineup but there's still some juice left in 2016 and some time to reflect on all of the dope sneakers that the Jumpman has released this year. 

Jordan Brand kicked of the year with the release of the white and red "Alternate 89" Air Jordan 4 on January 2nd, followed by an entire month of nothing but never before seen Air Jordans, including the Just Don x Air Jordan 2 collab. That collaborative Air Jordan 2 was pristine but like most collabs they were super exclusive and weren't a general release- so I'm leaving them off this list. 

The purpose of this recap is to showcase all of the best Air Jordans that dropped this year that the common man (with $200 to spend) actually had a real chance at getting their hands. Not just the limited edition rare pairs that are fetching $1,000 at Flight Club.

There were plenty of dope Air Jordan 1s, 4s and 11 Lows but not everyone can make the cut. Hit the slides for our picks of the Top 10 Air Jordans of 2016. Let us know which releases were your favorites.

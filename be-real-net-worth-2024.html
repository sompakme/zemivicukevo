Understanding Net Worth

Before diving into the specifics of Be Real’s net worth, it’s important to understand what net worth means. Net worth is the value of all assets owned minus the liabilities owed. For an individual, this includes cash, investments, properties, and other valuable possessions, minus any debts or obligations. For a company, net worth, often referred to as shareholders’ equity, includes assets such as cash, real estate, intellectual property, and more, minus liabilities like loans and accounts payable.

Components of Net Worth

  • Assets: Everything of value that is owned.
  • Liabilities: All debts and obligations that need to be paid.
  • Equity: The residual interest in the assets after deducting liabilities.

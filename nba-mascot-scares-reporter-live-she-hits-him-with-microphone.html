NBA mascot scares reporter live, she hits him with microphone

Brooke Olzendam, a reporter for the Portland Trail Blazers, suffered a tremendous scare when her broadcast was interrupted by 'Rumble The Bison', the mascot of the Oklahoma City Thunder.

The reporter's reaction to the furry bison's unexpected appearance on the court at the Paycom Center in Oklahoma City is... terrifying. She was so scared that she actually punched him with her microphone square in the face. Good job the person was safely protected by the mask!

The good thing for the Rumble mascot is that his Thunder ended up winning the match 123-121 with a winning basket by Shai Gilgeous-Alexander (35 points).

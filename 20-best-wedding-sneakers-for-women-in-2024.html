20 Best Wedding Sneakers for Women in 2024

1

Best Collab

Cecilie Bahnsen x Asics Sportstyle GT-2160

Cecilie Bahnsen x Asics’ much-anticipated sneaker drop is the special piece that will offset any wedding dress and provide maximum comfort while doing so. Slip into these silver stunners, which take on a slight Mary Jane shape and are trimmed with signature detailing, like the floral straps. They’ll also look great styled with any piece from Ssense’s new bridal collection.

Sizes: 6–11

Colors: Silver, black

2

Best Retro-Inspired

Golden Goose Superstar Glittered Leather Sneakers

If you love a worn-in, vintage-inspired sneaker, Golden Goose’s Superstar style will be the perfect match for your big day. With notes of white and silver and even a baby-blue star, this glittery sneaker can easily represent your “something blue.”

Sizes: 35–42 IT

Colors: White

Most Feminine

Alexandre Birman Clarita Leather Bow Sneakers

Alexandre Birman’s infamous Clarita bow goes the casual route in sneaker form with this slip-on shape. Picture this with everything from a tulle ball gown to a sparkly after-party minidress, made for dancing into the wee hours.

Sizes: 5–12

Colors: White

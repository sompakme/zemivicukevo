BBN Nina Reveals Desire to Name Son Miracle After Tough Labour: People Would Think I Was Obsessed

  • BBNaija star Nina Ivy has finally opened up on her traumatising childbirth experience after four years
  • Taking to her Instagram stories, the reality TV star detailed how she suffered a series of complications while giving birth
  • Nina also admitted that she wanted to name her son Miracle after all she went through, but she opted for something else

BBNaija Double Wahala star Nina Ivy has shared her traumatic childbirth experience on social media.

On her Instagram stories, the mum of one shared how she went through a lot of complications four years ago during the birth of her son.

Nina showed off the scar on her belly after she woke up from a coma two weeks after her son’s birth. She explained that there was fear that something was wrong with her intestines and she had to feed on a tube. The reality star explained that she later tattooed the scar in 2024.

In another post, Nina added that she had seriously considered naming her son Miracle, but she was worried that people would think she was obsessed with her then-boyfriend while on the BBNaija show.

The TV star went on to share photos while explaining that she was on two life support machines as well as a feeding tube. Nina said she had anaemia, coronavirus, preeclampsia, hallucinations and even heart disease.

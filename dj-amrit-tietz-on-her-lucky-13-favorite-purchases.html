DJ Amrit Tietz on Her Lucky 13 Favorite Purchases

Chanel 22 Chain Hobo Bag From Rebag

“Forget the stock market—this is the real investment that will appreciate over your lifetime! I always buy my designer resale. It’s way more cost-justifiable, and I always make sure to do it through a trusted seller with a solid verification process, so I know I’m getting the real deal. I prefer this hobo style to the traditional flap bag because it holds more and and can comfortably fit a laptop, all my essentials, and even Frankie’s stuff too. #Mumlife.”

Kimaï Lab-Grown Diamond Hoop Earring

“I wear this set every day. They’re distinct enough to be noticed, but discreet and not so precious that they can’t be worn daily. I know this is a polarizing take as they’re lab-grown, but I love that these are responsibly sourced and conflict-free!”

Lenys World Candy Apple Velvet Slipper

“Investing in quality homewear has tremendously improved my quality of life! I work from home, so they get a ton of action … and I’m all about that cozy, cushy mum life. They’re handmade in Italy and chic enough that you can get away with wearing them around the block.”

Jacques Marie Mage Molino 55 Sunglasses

“If JMM has a million fans, I’m one of them. If JMM has one fan, then I’m THAT ONE. If JMM has no fans, that means I’m dead! But seriously, I’ve never met a pair of JMM glasses I haven’t loved. I really like their tinted styles, because you can get away with wearing them indoors.”

Choose Love Bundle of Warmth

“Most of us have everything that we need, but for those who don’t, a little really goes along away. Choose Love is an organization that helps support refugees and displaced people all over the world. Starting from $15, you can buy essential items like sleeping bags, kids supplies, and a hot meal directly for those in need. They also just opened a holiday pop-up at Rockefeller Center if you want to support them in person and see the impact of their work.”

Marantz TT-15S1 Turntable

“As a DJ, I have a civic duty to include a recommendation that would enhance your musical journey, and my go-to is always Marantz. These are trusted audio devices with guaranteed high performance. I love their sleek and minimal aesthetic, especially in smaller NYC living spaces. I highly recommend their turntable-and-amplifier combo, for a best-in-class listening experience.”

A Vacation at Palm Heights, Cayman Islands

Palm Heights Grand Cayman

“I am a firm believer in investing in experiences, and Palm Heights in Grand Cayman is by far the best of the best! Chances are you’ve seen their infamous striped yellow-and-white towel loungers on Instagram and saved it as travelspo. I’ve spent the last two years playing their big New Year’s party, so we’ve always gone down and spent holidays there. The whole experience is incredibly well thought out and considered, from little sweets, book recommendations, and notes they drop into your room, to having great daily programming and food options on site. They also just opened their spa, which is a godly experience. I love vacations where you’re so fulfilled, you don’t even have to step off the property.”

Doona Car Seat and Stroller, Midnight Edition

“Every parent needs this stroller. It is my number one most recommended item for new parents. It is an all-in-one convertible car-seat stroller. You don’t need a base, so even when traveling, you can strap it in with the belt. The bag also clips into the stroller—it’s beyond life-changing. I’m going to be bummed when Frankie grows out of this.”

Byredo Tulipmania Hand Wash

“I have always been a fan of Byredo’s scents, and their hand care products are a nice, elevated touch for the bathroom. This is a newly discovered scent for me. I actually found it on a recent dining experience, and it left such an impression on me that I went home that night and ordered it for myself! I also really like their Cotton Linen candle for the home.”

Rocky’s Matcha Ceremonial Blend Matcha

“I quit caffeine while pregnant and never looked back! While I still love to get a daily matcha at Blank Street, I also like to make a second matcha from the comfort of my home. Rocky’s Matcha is a ceremonial-grade matcha from Japan. I never thought I’d become a matcha girlie, but he really converted me. I even own a whisk!”

Arket Ankle Boots

“I am a big fan of everything Arket is doing right now. Their prices are always reasonable, and they make an easy entry to shopping sustainable. Products are made with eco-friendly and recyclable materials, and each item has a fully transparent supplier policy. The only catch is they don’t deliver directly to the U.S. BUT you can purchase most items from third-party retailers like Yoox. They have an awesome kids section and are always ahead of the curve when it comes to trend-forward pieces, like these metallic ankle boots.”

Nordic Knots Big Buds Area Rug

“Frankie has just started making moves, so we are covering every possible surface known to mankind to soften the tumbles. This is how we ended up acquiring not one but TWO area rugs from Nordic Knots. I’m usually a ‘solid-color rug’ kinda gal, but this collaboration with Giancarlo Valle is a fun way to add some character to a room.”

Love Wellness Bye Bye Bloat

Now 50% Off

“I’m not sure what witchcraft has gone into this product, but it really works. I’ve always struggled with bloating, from hormonal to postpartum—pretty much my entire life. Plus, I love to eat. (I know I could cut back on the pasta and pizza, but who wants to do that?!) I keep this in my daily rotation for traveling, holiday season, and those indulgent dinners!”

Halie LeSavage is the fashion commerce editor at Harper's BAZAAR. Her style reporting covers everything from reviewing the best designer products to profiling emerging brands and designers. Previously, she was the founding retail writer at Morning Brew and a fashion associate at Glamour.

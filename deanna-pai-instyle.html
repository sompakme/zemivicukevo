Experience

Deanna Pai is a freelance beauty writer, editor and copywriter currently based in New York. She's written about beauty and wellness for Vogue, Vanity Fair, New York Magazine, Cosmopolitan, Glamour, Better Homes & Gardens and more. Deanna has been a beauty writer and editor for more than a decade, yet her freelance projects have gone well beyond the beauty realm. She's done everything from leading the Glamour commerce team through fast growth and skyrocketing revenue to implementing SEO strategies across the Tampax website. She even managed and judged the Glamour Beauty Awards from 2020 to 2022. Over the years, she's sharpened her skills as a freelance beauty copywriter with established brands like Lancôme, Aveda and Living Proof as well as viral startups such as Cocokind and Glow Recipe. She's also served as an editorial consultant for Covergirl, Nexxus and Thayers Naturals.

Education

Deanna has a B.A. in English Literature from Northwestern University.

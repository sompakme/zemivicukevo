LONG Island Medium star Theresa Caputo celebrated the New Year in quite the outfit in a new photo.

The 55-year-old has always taken pride in her bold looks, and she dressed the part on the holiday, wearing a black skirt covered in multi-colored polka dots.

Theresa shared a picture of herself welcoming in 2022, standing next to her son, Larry Jr., who displayed his own style by sporting a pair of pants in two different colors.

She captioned the photo simply: "We're ready for 2022."

In an earlier post, Theresa offered warm new year's wishes to her fans, as her bedazzled dress glimmered in the light.

She said: "Can you believe how fast 2021 went? I mean, that's exactly how I'm feeling. I feel like it went by like that (snap).

"But I want to wish you all a very blessed 2022. All of the health and happiness for you and your family."

The Long Island Medium star went on to thank everyone for their support, especially during "these tough times."

STYLE CHILD

The reality star has always had a unique sense of style, including her signature bouffant hair.

Earlier in the night, she shared a snap of herself preparing to go out with friends.

She posed alongside three other women, writing: "Night out with the girls."

The psychic wore her hair in her signature big blond style and donned an oversized green sweater with thigh-high leather boots.

She accessorized with a lime green purse.

Fans were keen on the look and wasted no time letting her know as one commenter wrote: "Love the boots."

Another chimed in with: "Now that's one pair of heeled boots!" while a third said, "Love the grinch bag."

HAIR APPROVAL

While the outfits won approval, fans haven't been as kind to Theresa's hairstyle.

The star is know for wearing her platinum blonde hair in extreme fashions, giving her look a sky high lift, and recently took to Instagram to showcase a perfectly coiffed partial updo, with side bangs and longer pieces that fell in waves past her shoulders.

Theresa gave the camera a soft smile as she captioned her post: "Holiday Party ready!" before thanking her hair, makeup, and clothing stylist.

However, fans were not enthused as they raced to the comments section to slam her new look.

"Hair extensions and a rats nest on the top, time for a makeover," one scathed, adding: "And caked-on way too heavy and too much make up."

Reese Witherspoon Wore the Foundation 65-Year-Old Shoppers Love

The Screen Actors Guild (SAG) Awards happened over the weekend, and the stars pulled out their best glam for the big night. From Selena Gomez’s wedding-like gown to Jennifer Aniston’s modern-day “The Rachel” haircut, there was a lot to admire. One of the standouts? Reese Witherspoon, who wore a fiery red dress (the color du jour) and stunned with a smooth, truly poreless complexion courtesy of Dior’s Forever Matte Foundation. 

According to a press release and Witherspoon’s makeup artist Kelsey Deenihan, the products (at least partially) responsible for her enviable complexion included the Dior Forever Matte Foundation and Dior Forever Skin Correct Concealer. (The former is the star of the show, in my opinion.)

Dior Forever Matte Foundation SPF 15

Dior Forever Skin Correct Concealer

The Dior Forever Foundation comes in two variants; Skin Glow and Matte. Witherspoon wore the latter, though both have a very dedicated fanbase. The core of the formulas is similar, too; infused with skincare ingredients, they both come in 42 shades and are reportedly transfer-proof with 24-hour wear. The foundations feature iris extract, which is antioxidant-rich and reduces the breakdown of hyaluronic acid and collagen, and rose extract to soothe inflammation. 

More than 11,000 five-star shoppers across retailers confirm that the Dior Forever Matte Foundation does in fact have all of these benefits in tow. A 65-year-old shopper with “senior skin” that has “flaws” said it “smooths everything out,” and added that it stays put even when they’re “really hot and sweaty.” Plus, the finish is so smooth and seamless, another shopper said they actually “prefer to apply it without a primer.” 

Head to Nordstrom to shop the Dior Forever Matte Foundation. If you’re looking for a more hydrating finish, try out the Skin Glow version, also available at Nordstrom.

What is the Tory Burch private sale?

The Tory Burch private sale is a sales event that happens only a few times each year. We’ve seen previous discounts of up to 70% off in the Private Sale. To join the sale, you will need to sign up for the Tory Burch newsletter so your email address can be added.  While signing up for the newsletter will grant you access to private sale events, it also means that you will receive a 15% Tory Burch coupon code.

Does Tory Burch have price adjustments?

Tory Burch does have a one-time price adjustment policy. If you purchased an item 14 days ago, you can get a price adjustment if it is now on sale. To make a price adjustment, contact the Tory Burch customer service team from their website.

Tory Burch runs a Black Friday sale every year, and this year is no exception. What is the Tory Burch returns policy?

If you wish to make a return, you can do so within 30 days of delivery. Your items should be unused, in their original condition, and with any tags still attached for a complete refund. It’s worth noting any final sale items - including Private Sale and Sample Sale - cannot be returned.

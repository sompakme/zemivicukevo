Euroz Pays Homage To Royce &amp; Cole On &quot;Boblo Boat Remix&quot;

Upon dropping off a remix to A$AP Rocky's "Fukk Sleep," Euroz has rounded out his surprise drop with another new remix. This time, he's looking to Royce Da 5'9"s AOTY contender Book Of Ryan, which was previously led off by the J. Cole-assisted "Boblo Boat." While the original evokes nostalgic themes of coming-of-age, Euroz opts to target his competitors, citing a lack of rapping ability as the dominant reason. "Shit don't go as planned, you call audibles," raps Euroz, "the c-notes they seem solid, origami fold."

While his Rocky remix finds Euroz feeling himself, this go-around finds him more focused, intent on delivering thought-provoking insight and lyrical gymnastics. Restrained by the instrumental's soulful nature, Euroz lets his thoughts emerge as a stream of consciousness, not unlike the single's original authors. For those seeking a more lyrical approach, look no further.

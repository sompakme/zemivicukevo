Who is Jim Kelly? | Wiki, Age, Bio, Net Worth, Career, Relationship,Family

Spread the love

Jim Kelly, whose full name was James Milton Kelly, left a lasting legacy as an American athlete, actor, martial artist, and athletic instructor. His dynamic career encompassed various roles, from the big screen to the martial arts arena. Let’s delve into the life and achievements of this remarkable individual.

A Multifaceted Career

  • Jim Kelly’s talents were as diverse as they were impressive. He was not only a martial artist but also an actor and athletic instructor.
  • He made his mark in the film industry with roles in movies like “Enter the Dragon,” “Black Belt Jones,” “Take a Hard Ride,” and many more.
  • His presence extended to television as well, as he appeared in the TV series “Highway to Heaven.”

Early Life and Education

  • Jim Kelly was born on May 5, 1946, in Paris, Kentucky, United States.
  • He pursued his education at Bourbon County High School and the University of Louisville.

A Tragic Departure

  • Jim Kelly’s life was cut short when he passed away on June 29, 2013, at the age of 67.
  • He battled cancer, specifically squamous cell carcinoma in his upper jaw, which led to his untimely death at his home in San Diego, California.

 

Remembering the Iconic ‘Enter the Dragon’

  • Jim Kelly gained immense recognition for his role in the iconic movie “Enter the Dragon.”
  • His contribution to the film solidified his status as a superstar and a martial arts legend.

 

Physical Attributes

  • Jim Kelly stood tall at 6 feet 1 inch and maintained a weight of 79 kg.

Family and Personal Life

  • Despite his busy career, Jim Kelly cherished his role as a father. He had a daughter named Sabrena Kelly-Lewis.
  • His romantic journey included marriages to his college sweetheart Marilyn Dishman from 1967 to 1968 and Marcia Bently from 1980 until his passing. He was also in a relationship with film actress Rosalind Miles from 1973 to 1976.

A Legacy of Net Worth

  • At the time of his passing, Jim Kelly had amassed a substantial net worth of approximately $3.7 million.

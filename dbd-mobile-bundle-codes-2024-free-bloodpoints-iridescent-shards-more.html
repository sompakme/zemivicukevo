How To Use Redeem Bundle Codes In Dead By Daylight Mobile

Ready to use your Dead by Daylight mobile code?

  • Open Dead by Daylight Mobile on your mobile device.
  • Click on "Shop" and enter it.
  • Click "Bundle Code."
  • Paste, or type in, your Dead by Daylight Mobile code. You can find codes to enter from our list below.
  • Press "Redeem."

    • If you've successfully redeemed the code, you will receive a confirmation notification and will receive the item(s) in your inventory.

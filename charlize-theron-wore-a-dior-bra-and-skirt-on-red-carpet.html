Charlize Theron Wore a Dior Bra and Skirt On Red Carpet

While promoting her new movie, Atomic Blonde, Charlize Theron skipped the traditional red carpet gown route and instead wore nothing but a bra and mini skirt to the Berlin premiere last night.

Getty Images

Of course, the actress didn't opt for just any old bra—she wore the white Dior logo version with a matching high-waisted skirt and white pumps. The branded Dior straps have been spotted everywhere this season—from the street style scene to your favorite bloggers' Instagrams and the red carpet.

For Theron, it was the perfect opportunity to show off her killer body which, in part, may be the result of all her intense stunt scenes from the film. And at 41 years old, the actress proves there's no age limit to rocking lingerie as outerwear.

Maryse Celebrates Her Birthday In Super Revealing Red Dress Photo Drop

Maryse is a former two-time Women’s Champion in WWE and the wife of The Miz. Despite her absence from regular WWE television, she occasionally appears alongside her husband. Known for her confident and charismatic personality, Maryse never hesitates to showcase her beauty and sex appeal, which is a major draw for fans. She has a big following and her fans always look forward to her appearances on WWE.

The French Canadian bombshell was largely involved with The Miz’s feud with Edge and Beth Phoenix. Once that feud concluded, she found herself being a part of The Miz’s feuds with Logan Paul and Dexter Lumis.

The mother of two took to her Instagram and uploaded a few photos of herself wearing a super revealing red dress, which showed off what fans wanted to see, as she celebrated her 40th birthday.

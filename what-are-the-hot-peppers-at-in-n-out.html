What is the heat level of yellow chilli peppers?

Yellow chilli peppers have a smooth texture and will provide a spicy kick to whatever dish they are added to. These peppers have a sweet peppery flavour and a level of heat that may range from 30 000 to 50 000 Scoville heat units, depending on the variety (comparable to tabasco and cayenne).

Which peppers have the highest concentration of capsaicin?

Comparing the concentrations of capsaicin in habanero peppers to other peppers from the genus Capsicum, a study published in the “Journal of Environmental Science and Health” in 2006 discovered that Capsicum chinense, the genetic species in which habanero peppers reside, has the highest capsaicin concentrations.

Mark Wiens Wiki - Biography, Age, Wife, Net worth, Height, Family, Lifestyle &amp; More

Mark Wiens (born 26 February 1986; Age: 36 years) is an American YouTuber, Food Blogger, and Author from Phoenix, Arizona, USA. He is well known for running a YouTube Channel and blog related to food and travel. Mark’s most-watched video is “Extreme Chinese Street Food – JACUZZI CHICKEN and Market Tour in Kunming! | Yunnan, China Day 4” with 23 million views as of 8th of June 2021, published on 27th of December 2017.

Mark Wiens’ Biography (Wiki, Birthday & Education)

Mark was born on the 26th of February, 1986 to his parents in Phoenix, Arizona. As of July 2021, he is a 36 years old blogger with a family of 3. Mark belongs to the Pisces sun group and follows Christianity as his religion. He belongs to the Asian American community.

Mark moved to Albertville, France when he was only five years old, from there his family moved to the Democratic Republic of the Congo, Africa where Mark made new friends, enjoyed new cultures and food.

From there Mark’s family moved to Nairobi, Kenya where he lived till high school. After high school, he came back to America and pursued a Global Studies Degree from Arizona State University.

Mark attended his first year of school in Albertville, France after which his family moved to DRC, Africa where he was home-schooled in DRC for three years. From DRC, Mark and his family shifted to Nairobi, Kenya, by this time he was in 5th grade and joined Rosslyn Academy.

After Mark’s high school from there, he returned to America where for a year he attended Glendale Community College and then joined Arizona State University and graduated with a Global Studies Bachelor’s Degree in 2008.

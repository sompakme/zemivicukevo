Controversial Street-pop Nigerian singer Afeez Fashola, aka Naira Marley, recently sparked massive reactions on social media with an unusual comment he shared on his page.

In his post, Naira Marley reposted a post shared online by a netizen who asked netizens some important questions about the singer.

After Naira Marley's reaction to the recently leaked Mohbad's autopsy report went viral Legit.ng reached out to the Chief Imam of Abeokuta's central mosque. He shared his thoughts about Naira Marley's post. He said:

"Not all curses work. But when they come from a place of genuine pain and hurt, they might have devastating effects. Also, curses don't tend to have immediate effects. Karma exists, and every wrong done to another person shall be returned in folds."

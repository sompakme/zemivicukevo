Faceless Love Episode 11 & 12:- Faceless Love is a Thai romantic comedy series that is based on the Japanese drama Rich Man, Poor Woman. It tells the story of Veekit, a young and successful CEO who suffers from prosopagnosia, a condition that prevents him from recognizing faces. He hires Mirin, a new secretary who changes his life and helps him overcome his challenges. The series is produced by GMMTV and Keng Kwang Kang Waisai, and airs on GMM 25 and Prime Video.

The series has 10 episodes in total, and it started airing on November 8, 2023. The last two episodes, episode 11 and 12, are scheduled to air on December 13 and 14, 2023, respectively. Here are some details about the upcoming episodes:

Recap of Previous Episodes

  • Episode 1: The First Encounter. Veekit meets Mirin for the first time when she applies for a job at his company. He hires her as his secretary, but he doesn’t remember her face. Mirin is happy to work with him, but she soon realizes that he has a problem with faces.
  • Episode 2: The Second Chance. Veekit fires Mirin after she makes a mistake, but he regrets it and asks her to come back. Mirin agrees, but she asks him to treat her better. Veekit tries to be nicer to her, but he still doesn’t recognize her face.
  • Episode 3: The Third Party. Veekit and Mirin go on a business trip together, where they encounter Manit, Veekit’s rival. Manit tries to sabotage Veekit’s deal and flirt with Mirin. Veekit gets jealous and protective of Mirin, but he doesn’t realize his feelings for her.
  • Episode 4: The Fourth Wall. Veekit and Mirin attend a party, where they meet Nara, Veekit’s ex-girlfriend. Nara is a famous actress who left Thailand to pursue her career in Japan. She acts friendly with Veekit and Mirin, but she secretly wants to get back with Veekit. She also knows about Veekit’s condition and uses it to her advantage.
  • Episode 5: The Fifth Sense. Veekit and Mirin get closer, and Veekit starts to rely on Mirin more. He also begins to notice her scent, voice, and touch, which help him identify her. Mirin is happy that Veekit is opening up to her, but she is also worried about Nara’s presence.
  • Episode 6: The Sixth Sense. Veekit and Mirin share their first kiss, which triggers Veekit’s memory of his past. He remembers that he had an accident when he was young, which caused his face blindness. He also remembers that he had a brother, who died in the accident. He confides in Mirin, who comforts him and promises to stay by his side.
  • Episode 7: The Seventh Heaven. Veekit and Mirin confess their love for each other and become a couple. They are happy and sweet, and they enjoy their time together. They also face some challenges, such as Veekit’s work pressure and Mirin’s family issues, but they overcome them together.
  • Episode 8: The Eighth Wonder. Veekit and Mirin celebrate their anniversary, and Veekit surprises Mirin with a romantic proposal. Mirin accepts, and they plan to get married. However, their happiness is short-lived, as Nara returns to Thailand and tries to ruin their relationship. She reveals a shocking secret that will shake Veekit’s world.
  • Episode 9: The Ninth Circle. Veekit is devastated by Nara’s revelation, and he breaks up with Mirin. He believes that Mirin betrayed him and lied to him. Mirin is heartbroken and confused, and she tries to explain to Veekit, but he refuses to listen to her. Nara takes advantage of the situation and tries to get closer to Veekit.
  • Episode 10: The Tenth Commandment. Veekit and Mirin are separated, and they miss each other. They also learn the truth about Nara’s secret, and they realize that they were manipulated by her and Manit. They decide to confront them and expose their schemes. They also decide to reconcile and get back together.

On a Murray riding mower, where are the safety switches located?

Murray riding lawnmowers are equipped with a safety cut-off switch, which is positioned beneath the seat. Designed to interrupt power to the mower’s engine if the operator leaves the seat while the mower is in operation, the pressure-activated switch is engaged by applying pressure to the switch.

Is it possible to mow using a Cub Cadet in reverse?

Cub Cadet tractors are meant to prevent the habit of mowing in reverse, which is inherently dangerous due to the nature of the equipment. Whenever the tractor’s key is set to the “normal mowing” position, the power take-off (PTO) will automatically disengage when the tractor is put into reverse.

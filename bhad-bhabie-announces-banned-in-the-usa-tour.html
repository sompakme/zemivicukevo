Bhad Bhabie Announces &quot;Banned In The USA&quot; Tour

Miss Danielle Bregoli A.K.A Bhad Bhabie is officially hitting the round to see all her "biches." The 14-year-old made famous from her Dr. Phil visit with her mother has announced her Banned From The USA Tour where she'll be hitting up North American cities and even hopping over the pond to Germany, Belgium, Paris, London and Amsterdam before closing things in Copenhagen.

Things kick off on April 14th in Santa Ana, California hitting up Dallas, Miami, Atlanta, New York, Toronto, Chicago and more. Peep the full schedule below.

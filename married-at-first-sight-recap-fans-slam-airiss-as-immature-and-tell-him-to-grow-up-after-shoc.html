More on Shaquille

Shaquille Dillon, 31, has made a career for himself in the fashion industry – he is the creator of Dapper Royalties, a bow tie business.

According to the “About Us” section of the company’s website, Dapper Royalties was founded in Baton Rouge, Louisiana in January 2014.

The site states: “Creator Shaq Dillon shared a photo on social media and his followers inspired him to continue to master his craft.

“Over the years, Dapper Royalties has made custom-made bow ties for all occasions, inclusive of Mardi Gras balls, HBCU Royalty, weddings, proms, and more.”

According to “Cast” under the ‘Married at First Sight’ section of the Lifetime website, Shaquille feels that he is “ready for marriage” because both his career and personal life are going well.

It continues: “He is open-minded, ready for love, and willing to put his fate in the hands of the experts.”

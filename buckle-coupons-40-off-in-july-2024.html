FAQs

Is there a Buckle free shipping option?

Yes! Buckle rewards members can access a whole host of benefits, and free shipping is one of them. If you aren’t a Buckle rewards member, explore your promo codes for Buckle free shipping options. 

Does Buckle offer a loyalty program?

Yes. Customers can join the free Buckle rewards program to earn 1 point per every $1 spent online. Earn 300 points to receive a $10 voucher to go towards your next purchase. 

Can I get a Buckle student discount?

Unfortunately, Buckle doesn’t offer a student discount at present. However, students can still make great savings by joining the Buckle rewards program and shopping with one of our Buckle coupons. 

Does Buckle have a military discount?

Yes - members of the US military qualify for a 10% discount on the Buckle website. Just verify your military status using SheerID to start saving. 

Where can I find a Buckle 15% off coupon?

If you opt to pay using a Buckle credit card, you will receive a 15% Buckle coupon code when you complete your first purchase.

Does Buckle accept returns?

If for any reason you aren’t happy with your purchase from Buckle, you can return it within 60 days of purchase. If you are returning swimwear or undergarments, you have 30 days from the date of purchase to return them. They must be unworn and with tags intact.

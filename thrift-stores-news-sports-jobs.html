Thrift Stores | News, Sports, Jobs

The following is a listing of nonprofit organizations that sell an assortment of used and donated goods. For changes or new listings, call 242-6335.

• Goodwill Retail Thrift Store, 250 Alamaha St., Ste. N-9, Kahului, 442-8989, Monday to Saturday, 9 a.m.-6 p.m., Sunday, 9 a.m.-5 p.m. Clothing, household goods, electronics, collectibles, toys and more.

• Habitat for Humanity, Maui’s ReStore, 1162 Lower Main Street, Wailuku, 242-1140, Monday to Saturday, 8:30 a.m.-4:30 p.m. Donations by appointment only. Furniture, new & used building materials.

• Ka Mana’olana Thrift Store, 1655 S. Kihei Road, Kihei, 875-2112, ext. 202, Monday-Wednesday, 9:30 a.m.-5 p.m., Closed Thursday, Friday 9:30 a.m.-5 p.m.

• Kala Iki Thrift Store, 100 Keokea Place (Kula Hospital grounds). Second and last Saturday of the month, 9:30 a.m.-noon. Drop off donations at the van.

• Maui Friends of the Library, Used Book Stores:

** Behind old Puunene School, 871-6563 or see www.mfol.org; Tuesday to Saturday, 9 a.m.-4 p.m. (hours vary).

** The Outlets of Maui, 900 Front St., Lahaina, across from Tommy Hilfiger, 10 a.m.-7 p.m. daily

** Queen Ka’ahumanu Center, down from Sears, Monday to Saturday, 10 a.m.-6 p.m., Sunday 10 a.m.-5 p.m.

• Po’okela Church Thrift Tent Shop, 200 Olinda Road, Makawao. Wednesday, 8 a.m. to Noon.

• Salvation Army thrift stores:

** 35 Hale Kuai St., Suite 314, Kihei, 875-8065, Monday to Saturday, 9 a.m.-5 p.m., Sunday 1-5 p.m.

** 131 Shaw St., Lahaina, 661-8827, Monday to Saturday, 9 a.m.-4 p.m.

• Secondhand Heaven, Makawao Union Church, 1445 Baldwin Avenue, between Paia and Makawao. Open 9 a.m.-2 p.m. Wednesdays and Saturdays. Welcomes donations 9-11 a.m. Mondays and Fridays; 9 a.m.-1 p.m. Wednesdays and Saturdays. No electronics, beds, automotive equipment or building materials please.

• St. Joseph Thrift Shop, 1294 Makawao Ave., 572-7652. Tuesday from 8 a.m.-noon and on the 2nd & 4th Thursday from 8 a.m.-noon.

• St. Rita Church’s Thrift Shop, 655 Haiku Road, 575-2601. Hours: Tuesdays 8 a.m.-noon; Sundays 9:30 a.m.-noon.

• Trinity Treasures Thrift Shop, 100 Kulanihakoi St., Kihei, 879-0161Thursday and Friday, 10-1, Saturday 8-12. Gently used clothing, footwear, accessories, books, household decor, sporting goods, housewares and more.

• Women Helping Women’s Revive Boutique, Wednesdays Donations Only 10 a.m.-2 p.m. Retail store hours : Thursday and Friday from 10 a.m.-4 p.m., Saturday 10 a.m.-2 p.m. Donations are also collected on Thursday and Friday from 10 a.m.-3:00 p.m. at 65 W. Kaahumanu Ave., Old Kahului Shopping Center, 495-0067. Gently used woman’s clothing, jewelry, household goods and small furniture.

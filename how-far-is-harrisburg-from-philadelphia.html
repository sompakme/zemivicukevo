Frequently Asked Questions (Because We Know You Have Them)

How to get from Philadelphia to Harrisburg by car?

Several routes will get you there. I-76 is a popular choice, but be sure to check traffic conditions before you head out.

How to get from Philadelphia to Harrisburg by train?

Amtrak offers regular service between the two cities. Tickets can be purchased online or at the station.

How long does it take to get from Philadelphia to Harrisburg?

Travel time can vary depending on traffic and mode of transportation, but generally speaking, it takes less than 2 hours.

Is Harrisburg worth visiting?

Absolutely! Harrisburg offers a mix of history, culture, and outdoor activities. Plus, the food scene is no slouch!

Can I fly from Philadelphia to Harrisburg?

Technically, yes, there is a small airport in Harrisburg. However, there aren't many direct flights from Philadelphia, and it might not be the most time-efficient option.

Does being tired affect a polygraph?

Make sure you are relaxed while taking a polygraph test, since nerves may lead to false results. Likewise, high blood pressure, drugs and alcohol, pregnancy, extreme fatigue and exceptionally tight clothing can all contribute to altering the results of a polygraph.

What if you are nervous during a polygraph?

Is there a possibility of failing the lie detector test when you are nervous? The lie detector test takes note of the physical responses which may include the heart rate or the breathing. Thus, provided that your feelings and emotions do not induce a direct physical response, there should be no problem with the test.

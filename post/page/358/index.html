Anyone But You is the latest romantic comedy to play with the "fake dating" trope, following two people who hate each other but pretend to be a couple while attending a destination wedding for spurious plot reasons. However, while Anyone But You may not win points for originality, it has already garnered attention for the sex appeal and believable chemistry of its two leads: Men's Health cover guy Glen Powell, who plays Ben, and Sydney Sweeney (Euphoria, The White Lotus), who plays Bea. Coco Chanel's best quotes

published on July 18, 2024
Gabrielle "Coco" Chanel's musings and incisive one-liners are as timeless as her Chanel designs and as long-lasting as her fashion legacy. The pioneering designer, who dominated Parisian haute couture for almost six decades, was also the first to use cotton jersey in womenswear. This revolutionised the oppressive fashion of the 19th century, liberating women from restrictive corsets and fussy petticoats with her elegant, more casual designs which were, primarily, comfortable to wear.

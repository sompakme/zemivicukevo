Operation True Love Chapter 90 Spoilers, Release Date, Raw Scan &amp; Where To Watch

Operation True Love Chapter 90: Operation True Love is a webtoon series that has a lot of fans who love its romantic comedy genre. It tells the story of Su-ae Shim, a high school girl who gets a smart flip phone named Jellypop from her locker. Jellypop says he can help Su-ae with her love life, especially with her cold boyfriend, Minu Kang, and her charming stepsister, Ra-im. But things get more complicated when Su-ae falls for Minu’s friend, Eunhyeok and finds out that Minu and Ra-im might have a secret relationship.

The webtoon series is created by Kkokkalee as the writer and Deuldeom as the artist. It is updated every Friday on the Naver Webtoon platform. The series is praised by readers for its adorable and hilarious characters, its surprising plot twists, and its sweet romance.

If you are a fan of Operation True Love, you might be curious about when the next chapter will come out, what will happen in it, and where to read it. In this article, we will tell you everything you need to know about Operation True Love Chapter 90.

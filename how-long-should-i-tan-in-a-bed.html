Why are my legs not tanning on sunbed?

    Why does my stomach tan more?

    While the number of melanocytes is virtually constant in people of all colors and in all regions of the body, the amount of melanin made by those melanocytes is different and is what determines skin color. So if your stomach gets tanner than your face, it's because the melanocytes there make more melanin.

    Does being wet help you tan?

    Misting your skin with water does not make you tan faster. This idea likely stems from the truth that being in water can make you tan faster. The reason why we tan faster in or above the water is because the water reflects the sun which in turn amplifies the sun's rays.

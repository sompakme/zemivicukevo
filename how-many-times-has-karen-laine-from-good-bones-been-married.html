How Many Times Has Karen Laine From Good Bones Been Married?

During Season 5 of "Good Bones," Karen Laine's husband, Roger, was diagnosed with cancer, per Cheatsheet. Between her busy filming schedule, Karen made it a priority to accompany him to every treatment appointment. It isn't known how long Karen and Roger have been married, but he started appearing on her Instagram account back in 2017 and is her fourth husband.

Karen's first husband was orthopedic surgeon Casey Starsiak, per Legit. The couple had three children together, including "Good Bones" co-star Mina Starsiak Hawk and her brothers William and CR. After Karen and Casey divorced, she moved on to her second husband, a man named Randy, per Cinemaholic. Karen and Randy had a daughter named Kelsy. They also divorced, and Karen moved on and married a man named Mick.

After her third divorce, she met and married Roger, whom she is still with today.

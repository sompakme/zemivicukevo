The 10 Best Nude Lipsticks for Black Women 2022

1

For Tan to Medium Skin

MAC Lipstick Matte in Velvet Teddy

Osbourne suggests those with tan to medium skin look for nude shades that are more of a rosy pink. "Think of taking your neutral pink lip and enhancing the color," he says. One of his picks is this pink-ish tan color from MAC that goes on matte and is a great neutral. 

2

For Tan to Medium Skin

Make Up For Ever Artist Liquid Matte Lipstick #109

You'll also want to consider undertones when looking for a good nude. If you have tan to medium skin, Osbourne sys to look for cooler shades of pink. Another pick of his is this liquid matte lipstick that glides on super smooth and lasts for hours. 

3

For Tan to Medium Skin

Marc Jacobs Beauty Le Marc Lip Crème Lipstick

This terra cotta blush shade is stunning and it applies creamy and smooth. It's also infused with seaberry and cocoa butter to nourish your lips.

4

For Tan to Medium Skin

Anastasia Beverly Hills Liquid Lipstick in Ashton

As for colors to avoid, Osbourne says to stay away from lighter pinks, as they they can come off as a powder pink and look more chalky. He also says you don't won't too go too dark as it could appear washed out or like you have no lips. This liquid lipstick will never let you down.

5

For Tan to Medium Skin

The Lip Bar Matte Liquid Lipstick

This fan-favorite liquid lipstick is the perfect deeper nude. It has a matte finish but doesn't dry out your lips—which is all we ask for from a liquid lip.

6

For Dark to Deep Skin

Uoma Badass Icon Matte Lipstick

Osbourne suggests that women with darker to deep skin tones lean toward honey tones. This light tan matte lipstick is one of his top picks.

7

For Dark to Deep Skin

Beauty Bakerie Salted Caramel Mocha

Osbourne also says those with dark to deep skin will want to opt out of pure beige tones, which can tend to appear ashy or chalky. This light mocha color looks gorgeous and won't wash you out. 

8

For Dark to Deep Skin

Mented Semi-Matte Lipstick in Dope Taupe

"Less is more when working on deeper skin because the lips tend to be two-toned already," Osbourne says. This taupe is a blend of brown and pink that is subtle enough to act as the perfect neutral for dark to deep skin tones. 

9

For Dark to Deep Skin

NYX Lip Lingerie in Teddy

Boosted with vitamin E, this brown lipstick with a cool grey undertone nourishes lips while leaving a long-lasting matte finish.

10

For Dark to Deep Skin

hourglass Femme Nude Lip Stylo #6

This mauve-y nude adds fullness and dimension to lips and also allows for precise lining.

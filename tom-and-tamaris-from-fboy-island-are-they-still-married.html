Tom and Tamaris from FBoy IslandAre They Still Married?

The intriguing premise of “Fboy Island” makes it stand out from the competition as an exciting dating reality show. The programme centres on three women and follows them as they interact with 26 male competitors in search of the ideal mate. Unbeknownst to them, the male participants are split equally between lovely guys and FBoys, who will break hearts for money. Throughout the season, the ladies go on dates with the men, and at the conclusion of each episode, they decide which one to get rid of. However, it should come as no surprise that the FBoys are competing on the show to take home the cash, while the nice guys are hoping to meet the love of their lives and split the reward with them.

The show’s ultimate goal is to determine whether love may cause FBoys to change for the better. It’s interesting to note that during “FBoy Island” season 2, when Tamaris Sepulveda, a 29-year-old account professional from New York, lay eyes on fitness enthusiast Tom Carnifax, sparks flew. The two bonded quickly and were even caught making out just a few days into the competition. Fans are curious to know if Tamaris and Tom are still dating as the cameras have been turned away. Let’s investigate to find out.

The Journey to Boy Island of Tamaris and Tom

The instant Tom entered the picture and introduced himself, Tamaris was smitten. She subsequently remarked how much she loved his endearing personality and knew she would get along well with him. Thus, it was only logical that Tom would be among the guys Tamaris favoured when it came time for her to give Brochalla her VIP cards. Even though they had a heart-to-heart at Brochalla and even got to know one another better, they did not at first appear to be the ideal match. Tamaris was also hesitant to start a relationship with one particular man because she had a lot of other alternatives.

As a result, Tamaris selected Jabriane and spent the entire second day with him when it came time for the females to select a date. They enjoyed themselves surprisingly, and other competitors like Jared and Mikey D. made it known that they were also keenly interested in the account executive. Even so, it appeared like Tamaris only had eyes for Tom because the two of them had a unique bond that was unmatched by many. When she finally did ask him out, the other people were really envious when they were seen having a passionate makeout session.

But when asked why she did what she did, Tamaris said that Tom made her feel very unique and that despite the occasional FBoy vibe, she couldn’t help but fall deeply in love with him. Things, however, abruptly changed when the show presented the season 1 runner-up, Casey Johnson. Tamaris was in New York working as an account professional when she saw Casey and realised she was missing out on something. Naturally, Casey did the same, and the two even spent a day together before sharing a loving kiss.

Naturally, Tom was dissatisfied with how things had turned out, but it appeared that he was not prepared to give up. He made every effort to repair his friendship with Tamaris and get to know her better in the days that followed. Unfortunately, she always had a strong physical desire to him, whereas with Casey, she always felt at peace. But when it came time for the contenders to divulge their status, Tom and Casey both described themselves as good individuals, which gave Tamaris confidence in her decisions.

Despite this, the account executive in New York found herself in a difficult situation right up until the last minute. To Tom’s dismay, Tamaris chose to eliminate Tom when she was given the option of giving up one of her finalists in exchange for FBoy Niko Pilalis.

Tom Carnifax and Tamaris Sepulveda: Still Dating?

Since Tamaris and Tom didn’t leave the programme together, their chances of reconciliation were already remote. The former identified as a “FGirl” on the programme and said she did not trust anyone, not even good people. The two also had good relationships with other “FBoy Island” contestants even after she eliminated Tom just before the finale, but their social media activity has been very minimal. Tamaris, on the other hand, seemed to have given up on him after discovering that her desire was primarily physical.

Tamaris and Tom returned to their regular life after filming was finished, and they haven’t shared many details about their present situation. As of right now, Tom has resumed his position as a fitness influencer and instructor and alternates between Waren, Ohio, and Florida. Tamaris is currently travelling back and forth between New York and Miami to develop her modelling career while also working as an account executive. Both also seem to be highly active on social media and like updating their fans on their daily activities. So far as we know, Tamaris and Tom are content with their own lives after going their separate ways.

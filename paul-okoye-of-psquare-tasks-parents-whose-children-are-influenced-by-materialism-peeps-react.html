Paul Okoye of PSquare Tasks Parents Whose Children are Influenced by Materialism, Peeps React

  • Singer Paul Okoye has expressed his displeasure at the rate young ladies are being advised to bill men
  • He noted that it is the negative influences from others who are materialistic that leads to this behaviour
  • The singer said the consistent demand that men should fund the lifestyles of ladies should be checked by parents

Singer Paul Okoye of PSquare, aka Rude Boy, has called the attention of parents to their young female children who believe it is the duty of men to fund their lifestyles.

He noted on his Instagram stories that it was wrong influence that caused this mindset and it could lead to what they did not bargain for.

His statement was a reaction to demands by some ladies that their partners have to fund their lifestyle. Besides, they have placed a certain amount on what they should be given before agreeing to go on a date with any guy.

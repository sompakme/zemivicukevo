Strictly fans make same complaint ahead of BBC centenary show

Fans took to Twitter

    Strictly Come Dancing fans have taken to Twitter to complain about the song choices for the upcoming show on Saturday, which is set to celebrate 100 years of the BBC.

    To mark the centenary, the celebrity couples will perform to either an iconic BBC theme tune or dance in tribute to one of the BBC's most loved services.

    Theme songs from shows such as and will also feature as well as ABBA's Waterloo in celebration of Eurovision, and The Ballad Of Barry and Freda, dedicated to BBC comedy icon Victoria Wood.

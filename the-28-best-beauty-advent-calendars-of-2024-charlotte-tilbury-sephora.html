The 28 Best Beauty Advent Calendars of 2024: Charlotte Tilbury, Sephora

Beauty advent calendar season is upon us, which means our favorite skincare, makeup, candle, and self-care brands have been busy creating a selection of glorious holiday collections for us to enjoy for ourselves and to gift to others. After all, nothing screams holiday joy like receiving a tiny gift every single day in December. 

If you're in the market for a beauty advent calendar, you likely fall into one of three categories. Most of the time, you’re already a huge fan of the brand and want to re-up your collection. Other times, the brand’s offerings are too pricey to buy separately and you’re looking to add a touch of something luxe into your routine. Or, you’re interested in a brand but aren't sure which products to try first—full-size products can be wasteful if you find that they don’t actually work for you anyway. 

The best beauty advent calendars solve all of these problems. They also make perfect gifts: Investment-worthy brands like Diptyque and Dr. Barbara Sturm's calendars make the perfect luxury gift for your mom, your sibling, or a friend.

Ahead, we broke down exactly what you’ll find in each of the best beauty advent calendars on the market—without spoiling the surprise of which product you’ll get on each day, of course—as well as how many days each calendar lasts.

Shop Deals on Beauty Advent Calendars

Under $50 Beauty Advent Calendars

Sephora Collection After Advent Calendar

This under-$50 advent calendar comes from Sephora’s in-house brand, Sephora Collection, and boasts 11 days of full-sized favorites from the cult-favorite line. The waterproof mascara included in the kit has over 200 five-star reviews and is hailed as a best-in-class option. “It not only thickens my lashes but lengthens them too,” wrote one reviewer. Also included are a powder blush, a red lipstick, and a hydrating lip mask, among others. 

Number of Products: 11

Sephora Favorites Mini Luxe Vibes Beauty Set

I used to obsess over the Sephora Favorites packages every single year. This advent calendar includes picks from top-selling brands like Tom Ford (check out our reviews of the brand’s best perfumes ahead of the holiday season) and PAT McGRATH LABS. A mini size of the Hourglass Veil Translucent Setting Powder is also included, and it’s top-rated for not looking cakey on the skin in the winter. “I never write reviews but this setting powder is so good that I want everyone to buy it,” wrote one reviewer. Better yet, the full-size version of that powder alone retails for the price of the entire calendar. 

Number of Products: 7

Under $100 Beauty Advent Calendars

Clarins 12 Days of Christmas Calendar

Longtime favorite skincare brand Clarins is offering another advent calendar this year. This time, the 12-day calendar is going for $79 and includes a few full-size products. Full-size options include the Lip Comfort Oil in shade 04 Pitaya as well as Beauty Flash Balm. Travel-sized options include the Total Eye Lift eye cream that promises to lift and de-puff the area, among others. Snag it now for less than $100, down from an original price of $215.

Number of Products: 12

The French Best of Beauty Skincare Advent Calendar

PSA for all of the biggest French beauty fans out there: you can now skip the step of booking your very fabulous vacation to Paris and order this 12-day advent calendar instead. It includes 12 deluxe-sized samples from brands like PAYOT, Yon-Ka and more, all wrapped up in a very adorable red box. Plus, you save 73 percent off of the regular price of $243 when you shop it now for less than $100.

Number of Products: 12

The 2023 George & Viv Beauty Advent Calendar

I love Anthropologie’s George & Viv advent calendars because not only are the full of cute holiday goodies, but the box is *so* festive-looking. 2023’s edition includes over $350 of product for less than $100, and includes a 24 deluxe mini-i mix of fragrance and beauty picks from brands like Sunday Riley and Elemis (who also has a dedicated advent calendar on this list—keep scrolling for that.) Highlights include the Supergoop! Unseen Sunscreen SPF 40 (one of our favorite sunscreens) and PHLUR’s cult-favorite Missing Person perfume which is always sold out. 

Number of Products: 24

Mala The Brand Advent Calendar: 24 Days of Mala

Holiday-scented candles make great gifts, but finding the best scent for the person you’re shopping for can be tricky. This one from Mala the Brand includes scents from across all different categories, including floral fragrances like the “Daybed” scent which includes notes of violet, rose, and cedarwood forest pick, and traditional fall scents like the “Pumpkin Latte” option that features notes of cinnamon, pumpkin, and vanilla. Or, if you’re a winter scent fan, check out the candle called “Cabin Fever,” which is comprised of notes like cypress, evergreen, and moss. 

Number of Products: 24

L'Occitane Holiday Advent Calendar Gift Set

If you’re someone who loves to travel (or if you’re shopping for someone who is constantly on the go), you know that travel-sized products you find at hotels or in the airport aren’t always reliable. This 24-day set from L’Occitane has you covered. It’s full of travel-sized versions of their best-selling body washes, hand lotions, and more. Some of my personal favorites include versions from the brand’s almond-scented line. 

Number of Products: 24

OPI Holiday '23 Nail Lacquer Mini 25 Piece Advent Calendar

There seems to be a new nail trend every week. Stay up to date with this 25-day nail set from OPI. All of the colors come from OPI’s limited-edition Terribly Nice Holiday 2023 collection and include shades like cherry red, lavender, and milky pink for your subtle days. 

Number of Products: 25

Revolution Beauty X Emily In Paris 12-Day Advent Calendar

Emily in Paris stans, rise up. This set made in collaboration with Revolution Beauty contains 12 days of picks inspired by the show, like fluffy fake eyelashes and a ruby-red lipstick named “Paris Berry Red” and a nude shade inspired by her hometown called “Chicago Pink Nude.” Oh—and it includes two eyeshadow palettes, a mini glowing face primer, a makeup-fixing face spray, and more. 

Number of Products: 12

Under $250 Beauty Advent Calendars

Lancer Skincare 12 Days of Glow Advent Calendar

This 12-day skincare-focused advent calendar from Lancer Skincare is full of products beloved by celebrities and beauty editors alike. Lionel Richie and John Legend both rely on products from the brand in their daily beauty routines. Designed around anti-aging and firming the skin, these products will give you a new look in the winter (and save you from the dull winter skin effect).

Number of Products: 12

Juliette Has a Gun Not an Advent Calendar

As the name might suggest, this advent calendar from fragrance brand Juliette Has a Hug is *not* like the others on your list. Instead of gifting this ahead of the holiday, this calendar is meant to be given on Christmas Day and opened between December 26 through to New Year's Eve. It gets its name from the brand's best-selling frangrance, Not A Perfume, and features a selection of products in the scent. With second-skin appeal and comprised of only one note—cetalox, which is normally used as a base note in other, layered fragrances—this is the gift for someone who doesn't normally wear perfume but wants to get into it.

Number of Products: 6

Peter Thomas Roth 12 Days Of Holiday Glow Calendar

This glowy kit from Peter Thomas Roth gets its name courtesy of all of the products included that promise to give you radiant skin this winter. Personal favorites like the Cucumber De-Tox® Hydra-Gel Eye Patches and the Cucumber Gel Mask Extreme De-Tox ™ Hydrator sit alongside best-sellers like the Pumpkin Enzyme Mask Enzymatic Dermal Resurfacer and the Instant FIRMx® Eye Temporary Eye Tightener. Say goodbye to dull, dry, cold-weather skin.

Number of Products: 12

LUSH Christmas Buds Advent Calendar

There is nothing nicer than a bath in the wintertime, especially when bath bombs from LUSH are involved. Plus, the calendar includes exclusives like te Snowman Bubbleroon and Chocolate Santa Bath Bomb.

Number of days: 25

Nudestix 12 Days of Nudestix - Advent Calendar

Neiman Marcus 12 Days of Fragrance Holiday Advent Calendar

Winter fragrances are the perfect gift, and this calendar from the team at Neiman Marcus includes *so* many of our favorite scents here at Marie Claire.

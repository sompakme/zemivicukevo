Hailey Bieber Shows Off Natural Freckles in Filter-Free Selfie

Hailey Bieber is a natural beauty. If you needed any more proof, direct your attention to the model's latest selfie.

The 24-year-old took to Instagram today to share a new, filter-free photo showing off her gorgeous, natural freckles. She opts for a simple white tank top and accessorizes with long, layered silver chain necklaces in the picture. Bieber didn't caption the photo, because, well, it doesn't need one.

This isn't the first time the model has shared a fresh-faced selfie on the 'gram. Here's a quick look back, just in case you need it.

When BAZAAR.com caught up with Bieber late last year, she revealed that when it comes to maintaining her skin's signature healthy glow, the key is going au naturel—as in, literally, turning to nature.

"My skin feels the best when I've been in the ocean," the model explained. "Salt water is the best skin healer for me, and I've found that nature can be the most soothing for my sensitive skin."

When she does put product on her skin, Bieber is choosy, going for only the cleanest and most natural options.

"My beauty philosophy is finding clean and nourishing products with ingredients from the earth that are backed by science," she added. "I've recently discovered this whole world of natural beauty that I love."

If that's the secret to getting skin like Hailey Bieber's, we're definitely in.

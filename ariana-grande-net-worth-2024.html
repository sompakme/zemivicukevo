Early Life and Career Beginnings

Grande’s musical career took off with her debut album “Yours Truly,” which topped the charts and set the stage for a series of successful albums. Each release, from “My Everything” to “Positions,” has contributed significantly to her net worth, with millions of copies sold worldwide.

COLLEGE sensation Olivia Dunne has stunned fans with her latest racy Instagram selfie.

The 20-year-old LSU gymnast and social media star is about to enter her senior year with the Tigers.

Currently enjoying her final summer break, Dunne shared an insight into her vacation with a bathroom mirror selfie from down in Bradenton, Florida.

She looked utterly stunning showing off her athletic physique in a black sleeveless crop top and black leggings.

This post came the same weekend she shared what appeared to be an insight into her preparations for next season from LSU's gymnasium on TikTok.

Smiling for the camera while mid-handstand on a balance beam, Dunne's clip left one fan wishing they "had this level of balance in my life."

For Dunne, this marks the latest step in a whirlwind year.

Having recovered from a foot injury in time to return to action in late February, she played her part as the Tigers reached the NCAA Women's Gymnastics Championships in April.

After finishing fourth, she went on to reveal she had modeled for Sports Illustrated's Swimsuit edition, before making an appearance on the red carpet at the Country Music Awards in Dallas in May.

During the course of the summer, she has also enjoyed a trip to the Amalfi Coast in Europe, watched on as LSU won the NCAA World Series in Omaha, and enjoyed trips to Nashville, Tennessee, and New York.

Dunne also made a television appearance during the ESPYs in July.

And in a recent interview with ELLE Magazine, she opened up about her career and plans for the future.

"I know it's coming to an end, so I'm trying to take in every single last memory I can, but I'm also excited to see what the future holds," Dunne explained.

She went on to say she does expect a move to the coast to be on the horizon, with Malibu, New York, and Florida as potential options.

"Probably I'll end up near a beach, I'm guessing.

"Then I can just honestly go back to what I started doing. I could flip around on the beach. I could. That's how it all happened. All of this."

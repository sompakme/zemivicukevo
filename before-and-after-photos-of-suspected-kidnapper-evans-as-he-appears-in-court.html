Before and after photos of suspected kidnapper Evans as he appears in court

Notorious billionaire kidnapper, Evans, looks slightly different as he appears in court barely 41 days after his first arraignment at the Ikeja High Court.

41 days after his last appearance at the Lagos state High Court, notorious kidnapper, Chukwudumeme Onwuamadike a.k.a Evans, appears to remain 'cool' despite spending some time behind bars.

Evans who has spent the last 41 days in a prison, appears to have only changed slightly as opposed to when he was in police custody.

While in police custody and other previous appearances, Evans was known to have a clean-shaven head, but pictures taken this morning, October 19, by Legit.ng, shows he has grown some hair on his head while maintaining the beardless look.

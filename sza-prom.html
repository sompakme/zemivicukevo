SZA - Prom

SZA's CTRL features a lot of raw, stripped-down R&B, but it also contains some gloss. "Prom," one of the project's immediate highlights, is a shimmering pop gem that feels slightly out-of-step with the rest of the album, amping up the tempo and keeping a tight, pop-influenced structure. SZA provides her own call-and-response vocals on the hook, making for a powerfully catchy chorus that you can't wait to return. 

CTRL was released last Friday through TDE and RCA after experiencing delays. It features guest appearances from Kendrick Lamar, Travis Scott, James Fauntleroy, and Isaiah Rashad. Listen to it here.

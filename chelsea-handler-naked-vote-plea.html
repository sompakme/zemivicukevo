Chelsea Handler Naked Vote Plea

In a revealing, literally, social media post, an almost naked Chelsea Handler urged her followers to vote in Tuesday’s midterm elections like their life “depends on it.” 

The politically active comedienne and vocal critic of President Donald Trump posted a video to Instagram Stories in which she outlined how she would be supporting California Democratic candidates Katie Porter and Gil Cisneros on Tuesday.

In the video, Handler appears to be getting dressed as she pleads with people to vote. 

Handler is no stranger to nudity on social media. In 2014, she tested Instagram’s posting policies when she put up a topless picture of herself riding a horse, mocking an infamous picture of Russian President Vladimir Putin. Handler slammed Instagram for removing the picture, claiming it was “sexist” and that she had “every right to show I have a better body than Putin.”

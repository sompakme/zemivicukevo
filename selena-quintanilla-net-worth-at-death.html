Introduction to Selena Quintanilla’s Legacy

Selena Quintanilla-Pérez, known mononymously as Selena, was an American singer, songwriter, spokesperson, businesswoman, model, actress, and fashion designer. Referred to as the “Queen of Tejano music,” her contributions to music and fashion made her one of the most celebrated Mexican-American entertainers of the late 20th century. Tragically, her life was cut short when she was murdered at the age of 23. Despite her untimely death, Selena’s influence has endured, and her financial legacy remains a topic of interest.

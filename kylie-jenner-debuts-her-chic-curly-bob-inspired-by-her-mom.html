Kylie Jenner Debuts Her Chic Curly Bob, Inspired by Her Mom

When it comes to hair, we can always count on Kylie Jenner to keep us on our toes. From the recent short-lived return of her King Kylie cotton-candy pink tresses, to her iconic blue ombré locks—and then there was even her platinum-blonde era—we never know what to expect. For her latest ’do, she surprised everyone with a big chop.

The Khy founder took to Instagram Friday to debut her new look with a sultry selfie. In the snap, Jenner smizes at the camera while showing off her short, wavy chocolate-brown bob with long bangs, which she styled in big, bouncy curls across her forehead. Drawing inspiration from the family matriarch, the makeup mogul captioned the post, “kris jenner is quaking.” The momager jokingly responded, “you’re not even the fart,”referencing the line from Ice Spice’s recent hit “Think U the Shit (Fart).”

Jenner graciously gave us more of her new chic, playful hairstyle with a second post just hours later. In this pic, she poses against a white background while wearing an ultra-cropped flouncy black satin bubble top by Helsa, with a matching figure-hugging, low-waisted skirt, along with a dainty gold bracelet, several chunky rings, and small chrome hoops. She reaches for her hair, which looks beautifully, gently tousled, and wears glowy, bronzy makeup complete with a matte mauve pink lip.

Her mother wasn’t the only person Jenner seems to be channeling with her new hairdo—her tresses also bear some resemblance to her beau Timothée Chalamet’s. His long, curly rich brown locks have become a trademark for him.

The Kylie Cosmetics founder and the Wonka star made their Golden Globes debut last month, where they coordinated in sparkly black ensembles and engaged in some sweet PDA.

Becky Lynch and Bianca Belair Etch Names in Guinness World Records at Esports Awards

Becky Lynch’s hard work in WWE has paid off in many ways, as she is a multi-time women’s champion. Lynch has proved just how good she is, as she is now a Guinness Book of World Records holder after her appearance at the Esports Awards.

The Esports Awards took place on November 30th, as the event was held at Resorts World, Las Vegas for the second year in a row. In case you didn’t know, the Esports Awards are considered to be the Oscars for the esports industry.

During the event, Becky Lynch and Bianca Belair made a surprise appearance, as they presented the award for ESports Content Creator of The Year at the event. Not just that, Belair and Lynch entered the Guinness Book of World Records for the largest video digital video game display, attempted by Booga, Belair, Lynch and Golden Boy.

Naturally, this is a major deal for The Man and the EST of WWE, even if they aren’t avid gamers. Regardless, we will have to wait and see what WWE has planned for Becky Lynch and Bianca Belair next.

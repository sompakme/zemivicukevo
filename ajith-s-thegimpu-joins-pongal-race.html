Ajith's Thegimpu Joins Pongal Race!

Kollywood star hero Ajith’s upcoming film Thunivu is releasing for Pongal 2023. Fans excitement has been building since the release of the film’s first official poster. The both singles from the film impressed the audience. Now Today makers delighted Ajith Tollywood fans with a surprising announcement.

Thunivu is also being released in Telugu states simultaneously under the title “Thegimpu.” Noted overseas distribution channel Radhakrishna Entertainments in association with IVY Productions has acquired the Telugu distribution rights of the film. The film will be released widely in Telugu states.

Many mega biggies have already announced Pongal release dates in Telugu and Tamil. Ajith’s Thunivu will now compete with major films in both industries. Ajith has a decent fan base in Telugu states, and the producers are confident that this will entertain the audience here as well.

Makers will soon kickstart promotions in Telugu states. The team behind Thunivu is currently deep in post-production. With Ghibran composing the film’s score, anticipation among fans is rising steadily.

With Thunivu, producer Boney Kapoor, actor Ajith Kumar, and director H Vinod have reunited for the third time following the success of Nerkonda Paarvai and Valimai. Nirav Shah is responsible for the film’s cinematography, edited by Velukutty.

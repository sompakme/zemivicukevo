Will Levis' 'ex' Gia Duddy crushes rumors they are back together... feels 'confused' after breakup

After three years of dating, Will Levis and Gia Duddy put an end to their relationship back in September. But over the past few weeks, rumors have been growing of a possible reconciliation after TMSPN, a Sports entertainment website, reported that the two are "back on good terms" and close to getting back together.

However, Duddy's latest TikTok seems to suggest otherwise. In her latest post on the video platform, the social media influencer talked openly about breakups, recognizing that she feels confused.

"What happened to cuffing season? Why is everybody breaking up right now? All of my friends have broken up with their boyfriends... what's happening? And it's not even like there's no dramatic breakups, everyone is just breaking up because it's not what's best for each other right now," Duddy said.

"Instead of summer being breakup season, it's fall and winter. I'm confused..." she concluded. Most fans seem to think that her comments are based on her own experiences, having broken up not long ago. Her comment about a relationship being just "not what's best for each other right now" was particularly eye-catching, and many believe she was referring to her own personal situation.

It remains to be seen if Levis and Duddy will end up getting back together, but right now it looks unlikely.

Ariana Richards Net Worth

Ariana Richards is an actress best known for her roles in the science fiction thriller Jurassic Park and the creature feature Tremors. She was born on September 11, 1979 in Healdsburg, CA and is remembered for her iconic role as Lex Murphy in Jurassic Park, where she escaped from dinosaurs, and as Mindy Sterngood in Tremors.
